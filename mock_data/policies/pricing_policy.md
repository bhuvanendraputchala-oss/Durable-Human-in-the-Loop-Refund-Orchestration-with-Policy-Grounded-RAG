# Pricing Policy

All listed prices are final at the time of purchase and include applicable taxes.
Price adjustments are offered within 7 days if the same item is discounted after purchase.
Promotional discounts cannot be combined unless explicitly stated in the offer terms.
The company reserves the right to correct pricing errors; customers will be notified before any charge adjustment.
International orders may incur additional duties and customs fees, which are the customer's responsibility.
Bulk order pricing requires a separate written agreement with the sales team.
