# Escalation Policy

Any unresolved issue after two agent interactions must be escalated to a senior support specialist.
Issues involving potential legal liability, fraud, or orders above $500 require mandatory supervisor review before resolution.
Escalated tickets must receive a response within 4 business hours.
Customers who request escalation at any time must be connected to a senior agent within 1 business day.
Agents may not promise resolutions outside defined policy boundaries without written supervisor approval.
All escalation decisions and outcomes must be documented in the ticket system for audit purposes.
