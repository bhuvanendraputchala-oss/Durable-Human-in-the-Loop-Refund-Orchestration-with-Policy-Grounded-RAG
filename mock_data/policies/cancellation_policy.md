# Cancellation Policy

Orders may be cancelled without charge within 1 hour of placement if they have not yet entered fulfillment.
Orders already in fulfillment or shipped cannot be cancelled; customers must follow the return process after delivery.
Subscription orders may be cancelled at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
Pre-orders may be cancelled any time before the estimated ship date for a full refund.
Custom or personalized orders cannot be cancelled once production has started.
Cancelled orders with pending charges are voided; refunds for completed charges post within 3–5 business days.
