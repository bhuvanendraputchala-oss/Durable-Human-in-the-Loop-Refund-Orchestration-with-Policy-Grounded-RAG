# Dispute Resolution Policy

Customers must first contact support to attempt informal resolution before initiating a formal dispute.
Formal disputes must be submitted in writing within 60 days of the transaction date.
The company will acknowledge receipt of a dispute within 2 business days.
A resolution will be provided within 15 business days of receiving all required documentation.
If the dispute involves a chargeback initiated with the customer's bank, the internal dispute process is paused pending the bank's decision.
Disputes related to fraud allegations are forwarded to the risk management team for investigation.
Decisions can be appealed once within 10 business days by submitting new evidence.
