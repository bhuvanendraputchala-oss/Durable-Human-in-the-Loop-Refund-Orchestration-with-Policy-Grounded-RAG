# Customer Data Policy

Customer personal data is collected solely for order fulfillment, customer support, and service improvement purposes.
Data is never sold to third parties.
Customers may request a copy of their stored data or request deletion within 30 days by contacting support.
Payment information is encrypted and stored in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
Order history is retained for 7 years to meet regulatory requirements.
Marketing communications require explicit opt-in consent and may be withdrawn at any time.
Data breaches will be disclosed to affected customers within 72 hours of confirmation.
