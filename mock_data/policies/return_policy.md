# Return Policy

Customers may return unused items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
Items must be in original packaging and in resalable condition.
Final-sale and digital items are not eligible for return.
Return shipping costs are covered by the company if the item is defective or incorrect.
For change-of-mind returns, the customer bears return shipping costs.
A return merchandise authorization (RMA) number must be obtained before shipping items back.
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.
