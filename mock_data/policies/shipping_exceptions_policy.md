# Shipping Exceptions Policy

Hazardous materials, perishables, and oversized items are subject to special shipping restrictions.
Orders to P.O. boxes are limited to standard mail carriers and may take up to 10 business days.
During peak seasons (November–January) delivery windows may extend by 2–3 additional business days.
Weather or carrier-related delays beyond the company's control do not qualify for automatic refunds but customers may request a shipping credit.
If a package is marked delivered but not received, customers must report it within 7 days; an investigation will be opened with the carrier.
Rerouting or address changes requested after dispatch may incur a $5 service fee and delay delivery by 1–2 days.
